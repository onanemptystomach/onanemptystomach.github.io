Listed below are some organizations that help to end world hunger. If you click on the images, you will be taken to the "Donation" page of that organization.


UNICEF
UNICEF is the United Nations Children's Fund. It helps to improve the lives of children living in the most impoverished conditions, including providing them with food, health care, and basic education.
WFP
The World Food Programme is an organization belonging to the United Nations that specifically targets on improving food security and addressing the issue of world hunger.
Mercy Corps
Mercy Corps is an NGO that aims to help impoverished countries that are currently suffering (not only hunger-wise) by improving their economies so that they are sustainable.
Rise Against Hunger
This organization aims to eradicate world hunger by 2030, and it currently distributing food and other resources to the countries in need of help.
Action Against Hunger
A global humanitarian organization, Action Against Hunger helps to alleviate the effects of hunger worldwide and to take action on the causes of world hunger by launching numerous programs in 47 countries.
The Hunger Project.
The Hunger Project is a NGO that aims to end hunger in places that are suffering from poverty or a lack of resources, including Africa, South Asia and Latin America.
CARE
CARE works to not only end world hunger, but to end poverty and to bring social justice to communities around the world.
Bread for the World
Bread for the World is a Christian organization that works to end world hunger by changing policies and conditions that cause countries to become impoverished.
Heifer International
Heifer Internation wants to end world hunger in an environmentally friendly way by giving communities and farmers livestock and training.
