WHAT YOU CAN DO TO HELP
Listed below are a few ways that anyone, even the general public, can help to eradicate world hunger. If everyone can work together, the future of our world will shine brighter than ever. (hover over the images too)




DONATE
Donate to organizations (in monetary value) that work to end world hunger (click the "Donations" button up in the header for more information.




ORGANIZE
Organize food drives, and donate the food to food banks. Also, organize meal packaging events, and send the meals out to organizations that can help to distribute the food.




BRAINSTORM
Brainstorm ways to help people who do not have direct access to food get the resources that they need.




ADVOCATE
Advocate for the cause. One way to show support is by using social media to spread the word. Also, influence the public policy, as the government policies have a huge effect on the lives of the hungry, including how resources are distributed.




VOLUNTEER
Volunteer for any organization that aims to eradicate world hunger. Types of volunteer work include packaging food, campaigning for womenâ€™s rights, and educating communities.
