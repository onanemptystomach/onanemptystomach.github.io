OUR LOGO
Our logo symbolizes the three main levels of hunger: chronic hunger, acute hunger, and no hunger, by depicting varying amounts of rice in each bowl. The bowl itself can be thought of representing the person's stomach, and the rice can be thought of as food in general. Therefore, the empty rice bowl symbolizes an empty stomach, a half-full rice bowl symbolizes a fed, but not well-fed stomach, and the full bowl symbolizes a stomach that has been fed adequately, or perhaps excessively. We chose rice as our food, because rice is the main symbol of health, life and nourishment.
OUR GOAL
Ellie and Vivian want to contribute to the eradication of world hunger. Since they are young, they hope to spread awareness of the issue and to provide a general lens for the public so that people can see the real truth, and feel more motivated to take action.
